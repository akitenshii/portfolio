About Me

Hi, I’m Gabriela Z, a college student and creative designer interested in digital media, animation, and visual storytelling. I enjoy working on projects that combine design and technology, from 3D modeling to video editing and interactive web design.

My work focuses on building clean, visually engaging experiences while learning new tools like Photoshop, After Effects, and web development. I like experimenting with layout, color, and motion to make my projects feel more dynamic and personal.

Outside of design, I’m interested in music, culture, and exploring new creative ideas that inspire my work.
